I am Lindon Francis, a twenty-one-year-old French web developer based in Montpellier, in France. As a self-taught developer, my curiosity and my motivation always push me to create new things, it allowed me to learn autonomy through web development projects and design I made. While animating websites I always keep an eye on performances to give the user the most enjoyable experience possible. As a freelancer i work with companies, agencies and individuals all over the world. Feel free to send me a message if you want to collaborate or just have a talk!
